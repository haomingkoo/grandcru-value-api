# Responsible Data Use Notice

This service is intended for personal/consumer price transparency and value comparison.

## Data Collection Principles

1. We only collect publicly visible product metadata required to compare prices.
2. We do not bypass authentication, paywalls, CAPTCHAs, or technical protections.
3. We do not collect personal user data from third-party websites.
4. We use bounded request rates and retries to reduce operational load on source websites.
5. We stop or adjust collection if requested by a site operator.

## Compliance Commitments

1. We review and respect each source website's Terms of Service and robots directives.
2. We implement user-agent identification and conservative rate limiting.
3. We cache and reuse results to avoid unnecessary repeated requests.
4. We provide source links so users can verify prices directly on the original site.

## Accuracy and Liability

1. Prices and availability can change at any time.
2. This service does not guarantee accuracy, completeness, or uninterrupted availability.
3. Users should verify final pricing and terms on the merchant site before purchase.

## Important

This notice is a technical policy statement that explains how this service is operated.
