How the Deal Score works

Each wine is scored 0 – 100 based on five components:

ComponentMaxWhat it measures
Retailer discount30 ptsHow much cheaper Platinum is vs Grand Cru. Bigger discount = more points.
Market discount30 ptsHow much cheaper Platinum is vs the Vivino market price. This catches “fake deals” where Grand Cru is just overpriced.
Wine quality25 ptsVivino community rating (0 – 5 scale). Higher rated wines score more.
Confidence10 ptsNumber of Vivino ratings. More ratings = more reliable signal.
Double-beat bonus5 ptsBonus when Platinum beats both Grand Cru and Vivino market price.

A score of 70+ means the wine is well-rated, well-reviewed, and genuinely cheaper on Platinum than both the competitor and the open market. Scores update with each data refresh.

Data sourced from Platinum Wine Club, Grand Cru Wines, and Vivino. Prices are in SGD. Vivino ratings and market prices are community-sourced and may vary.
